About Melany

Melany grew up in a small farm town called Marsing in rural Idaho. Melany is the second of three children. Melany was the first Schultz girl in a long line of boys. Melany has an older brother Jason and a younger sister Katie who both live in Caldwell, Idaho. Melany has eleven first cousins and grew up calling all adults Uncle or Aunt – everyone was family to Melany. Melany moved to Reno in 2004 and has made it her home. Family is very important to Melany and she especially treasures spending time with her grandparents, Shirley Floyd, Ed Schultz and Sadie Schultz. Growing up Melany spent a lot of time with her grandmothers. There is a close bond with them that goes beyond being family.
