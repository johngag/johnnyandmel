About Our Wedding

John and Melany are getting Married!

The wedding will take place in John’s hometown of Nevada City, California. http://www.nevadacitychamber.com/ Saint Canice Catholic Church will be the site for the nuptials mass ceremony. The church is located at 317 Washington Street. http://www.stcanice.com/ Immediately following the ceremony the bride and groom will slip away with their families to take a few photographs. While they are taking their pictures you are invited to a cocktail hour. The cocktail hour and dinner reception will be housed at the Miners Foundry Cultural City located at 325 Spring Street. http://minersfoundry.org/ Upon the arrival of John and Melany dinner will begin followed by festivities and dancing the night away.
