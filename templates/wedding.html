Bridesmaids & Groomsmen's

  • Kim Carter, Maid of Honor
    Kim, who is affectionately known as The Matchmaker (along with her husband Adam) introduced Melany and Johnny. Kim and Melany worked together for several years. They have enjoyed so many great times and coffee-date-lunches over the years. So many happy and fun times like getting in trouble for talking too much in the back of a spin class ☺ Kim attended college at UNR and currently lives in Reno, Nevada with her beautiful daughter Kailyn (15 months), and her husband Adam.
  • Kayley Peacock, Maid of Honor
    Kayley and Melany hit it off the first time they met. Two peas in a pod! Kayley and Melany were roommates for one wonderful summer and had some great adventures. Kayley is truly the best roommate ever. Kayley lives in Spokane Washington with her husband, Nick, and their son Jack Thomas (6 months). Melany and Johnny are honored to be Jack’s godparents and cherish him dearly.
  • Katie Schultz, Bridesmaid
    Katie is Melany’s younger sister and is a legal secretary for a couple of attorneys in Nampa, Idaho. Katie and Melany have always been close sisters and good friends. This probably had something to do with being dressed like twins for most of their childhood. At one point Melany’s family was afraid Katie would never learn to walk because Melany carried her all the time as a kid. Katie lives in Caldwell Idaho where she has her own home and continues daily to make her big sister proud.
  • Sara McClintick, Bridesmaid
    Where to begin? Hee hee hee! Friendship bracelets, roller skating parties, bicycle adventures, walkie-talkies, mud ball wars, puff-paint-besties-shirts , and wading to the forbidden island to go fishing. Driving the farm truck at age six, horseback riding, tree climbing, camping trips and sneaking beer. Growing up with Sara and spending the endless summers together on our family farm was the best of times. Lifelong best friends, Sara and Melany were always together, so much so that they are like sisters. Sara and Melany grew up together in the small town of Marsing, Idaho. Sara now lives in Nampa Idaho with her son Curtis (10), step-daughter Alexis (16), step-son Tyler (14) and her husband Scott.
  • Christina Gagliardi, Bridesmaid
    Christina is Melany’s godmother and Johnny’s oldest sister. Melany and Christina spend a lot of time together and have because close friends over the past few years. Christina introduced Melany to Zumba and the art of Bunco. Zumba has turned into an abs-only workout for Melany who spends the majority of the class time laughing since she is either two steps behind or heading the wrong direction. Christina is always up for a good time and an impromptu dance party. Christina lives in Reno, Nevada with her two children, Matteo (7), Natalia (4) and her sweet husband Rick Schettler.
  • Erica Orme, Bridesmaid
    Erica is Melany’s craft and décor icon. Erica finds time amidst being a wife and mother of four to sew, bake, decorate and throw the cutest parties. Erica is one of those people you will meet and feel as if she has always been a part of your life. Erica never ceases to amaze Melany; Erica is truly a modern day Super Woman. Erica always has a smile on her face and a hug waiting for you. Erica lives in Folsom, California with her children Gio (4), Khai (3), Avennie (3) Keaton (six months), and her wonderful husband Brad.
  • Melissa Sherman, Bridesmaid
    Melany and Melissa worked together for several years at an international engineering firm. Melissa moved on to a wonderful locally owned and operated consulting and permitting firm and later recruited Melany. Melissa and Melany still enjoy working together and sharing belly laughs. Melissa and Melany have had many adventures together over the years. Melissa was always there for Melany through hard times and happy times and for that will be eternally grateful. Melissa lives in Reno, Nevada with her daughter Sierra (3) and her husband Jon.
